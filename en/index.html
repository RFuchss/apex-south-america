Who we are?

At Apex South America, we specialize in the wholesale of machinery, equipment, and related materials, as well as the sale and manufacture of motor vehicles. Our mission is clear: to offer high-quality products and services that drive progress in the automotive industry and other key sectors.

Headquartered in Coquimbo, Chile, we are strategically located, facilitating access to both local and international markets. The region, known for its stability and openness to trade, offers a favorable environment for growth and investment.

At Apex South America, we have witnessed and participated in the strengthening of trade ties between Chile and Asia, especially with China. Our strategic presence in the region has allowed us to build solid relationships, becoming a key partner for companies looking to expand in South America. This direct market experience opens doors to opportunities for collaboration and mutual growth.
